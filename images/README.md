## Where to Find Free Images:

### Wikimedia Commons (Recommended)
Visit https://commons.wikimedia.org and search for each celebrity's name. Look for images with Creative Commons licenses.

### Other Free Sources:
- Wikipedia articles (check image license)
- Official social media accounts (with permission)
- Press releases and promotional materials (with permission)

## Image Requirements:

- Format: JPG or PNG
- Recommended size: 400x400px or larger (square aspect ratio preferred)
- Quality: High resolution for best display
- License: Ensure images are free to use or you have permission

## Note on Copyright:

Always ensure you have the right to use any images. When in doubt:
- Use Wikimedia Commons images (usually CC licensed)
- Get permission from copyright holders
- Use official promotional materials with proper attribution
- Check license terms before using
